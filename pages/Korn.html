Korn is an American nu metal band formed in 1993 in Bakersfield, California. They pioneered the nu metal genre by blending heavy metal riffs with hip-hop, funk, and industrial influences, featuring downtuned guitars, aggressive rhythms, and Jonathan Davisâ€™s distinctive vocal style.
